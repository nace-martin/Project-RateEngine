Terms and Conditions

Below are the terms and conditions governing all services provided under this rate card. Please review them carefully to understand your responsibilities and obligations.

General Terms

  • All rates are subject to 10% GST.
  • Rates are subject to change with or without prior notice.
  • EFM does not provide insurance coverage. It is the importer's responsibility to insure the cargo.

Payment Terms

  • Disbursement invoices must be paid upfront prior to EFM making payments on behalf of the client.
  • Clients who are not eligible for credit terms must pay all charges in full before delivery. These shipments are strictly Cash on Delivery (COD) and will not be released until payment is received.

Surcharges and Fees

  • An overweight surcharge applies for shipments exceeding 200 kilograms as outlined in the rate card. Contact us for shipments exceeding 2 tonnes.
  • Storage fees apply for cargo stored in EFM's Bond beyond the free storage period (as detailed in the rate card).

Regulatory and Compliance

  • All customs clearance and duties are subject to the classification and assessment by PNG Customs. Any discrepancies will be billed or refunded accordingly.
  • GST exemption letters must be provided by the consignee for exemptions to apply.
  • EFM is not liable for delays caused by regulatory inspections, customs processes, or other third-party actions.

Disputes and Liability

  • Any disputes regarding rates or services must be raised in writing within 7 business days of the invoice date.
  • EFM is not responsible for delays or charges resulting from unforeseen circumstances, including natural disasters or strikes.
